Happy Anniversary Ocean!

My dear love, how you shine so bright,

No matter the distance we sit apart,

Our hearts meet to dance in the dead of night,

I gaze out my window to admire our art.


The way I long for you after you shut your eyes,

Your lovely voice; my only lullaby,

My only warmth in this world of ice,

I'd cry a river so you could fly.


But when morning meets; your spectrum greets,

My eyes filled with your rainbow shine,

Your words make my heart skip beats,

How could I ever deserve you, my divine?


Take my hand and let's promise our devotion,

Let's let our love embrace forever in our ocean.
